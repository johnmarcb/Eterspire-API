Spiderfang Gear

From Eterspire Wiki

Tier 4

Dropped by Uma'gaga, the Recluse, a level 20 Remnant found in Spider's Nest.

Spiderfang gears share the same values as Bloodchrome gears. Spiderfang, being a boss gear, has no negative or penalty rolls.

.
